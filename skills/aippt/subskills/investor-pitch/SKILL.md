# Investor Pitch

Use this scene pack before the generic workflow when the request is a new fundraising deck.

## Route when

- fundraising deck / pitch deck / VC deck / startup deck
- seed or Series A investor presentation
- venture-facing story with market, traction, team, and ask

## Defaults

- Audience bias: seed investors, venture partners, investment committee
- Page budget: 10-16 pages, default 12
- Style preset: `tech`
- Delivery default: `brand_ready_assets`
- Story arc: `anchor -> proof -> proof -> bridge -> proof -> closing`

## Required sections

- market opportunity
- product thesis
- traction
- business model
- go-to-market
- team
- funding ask

## Evidence bias

- use investor-grade metrics with dates, comparable definitions, and first-party traction when available
- use reputable market sources when external sizing or benchmark claims are needed
- separate vision slides from proof slides

## Review bias

- prioritize `chart_legibility`, `citation_visibility`, `hierarchy`

## Near misses

- product launch or keynote decks -> stay generic unless the ask is truly fundraising
- internal sales or partner pitch decks -> stay generic or company-intro
- critique-only or redline requests -> route away from this scene pack
